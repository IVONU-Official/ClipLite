# ClipLite 1.0.0 – Compress Videos Without Loss

**Fast, modern, and professional video compression tool.**  
Reduce file sizes up to 90% while maintaining stunning quality.

---

## Supported Platforms
- **Windows 10/11**
- **Linux** – DEB and RPM packages

---

## Features
- **Drag & Drop Interface** – Simply drag your video or click to browse
- **Original Video Details Displayed** – Resolution, frame rate, bitrate, duration
- **Custom Compression Settings** – Adjust resolution, frame rate, video/audio bitrate, and quality (Best 🏆, Better 👌, Good 👍)
- **Extreme Speed** – Optimized FFmpeg engine with hardware acceleration support
- **Modern Interface** – Clean dark-themed UI, easy-to-navigate workflow
- **Privacy First** – All processing stays local. No cloud uploads, no privacy concerns

---

## Why Choose ClipLite?
- **Engineered for performance and ease of use**
- **Extreme Speed** – Hardware-accelerated FFmpeg
